Birth & Early Life:
Sundar Pichai was born on June 10, 1972, in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. His full name is Pichai Sundararajan. He grew up in a modest two-room apartment with his parents and younger brother. His father was an electrical engineer and his mother was a stenographer.

Education:
Sundar Pichai completed his schooling from Jawahar Vidyalaya, Ashok Nagar, Chennai. He then did his B.Tech in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur. Later, he went to the United States for higher studies. He earned an M.S. in Material Sciences and Engineering from Stanford University and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

Career Start:
Sundar worked at McKinsey & Company and other tech firms before joining Google in 2004. He initially worked on the Google Toolbar, and later played a key role in the development of Google Chrome.
